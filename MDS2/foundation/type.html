Typography

Fraunces + Nunito Sans

The typographical hierarchy makes use of Fraunces and Nunito Sans. Fraunces is a serif font that with minimal flair, and it pairs well with Nunito Sans, which acts as a readable sans-serif typeface. Together, they are arranged in Major Third Scale with a base font size of 16px.
