Cards

This page contains cards that will link to examples of design work. They will include a project title, image, description, and link to view the work in greater detail.

Example

The cards should be responsive. Adjust the browser to view the different sizes.

Accessibility

Ensure that all images have the appropriate alt text and that the buttons are labeled correctly to reflect the page they will direct to.

Usages

Cards must include an image, project title, description, and button to view.
